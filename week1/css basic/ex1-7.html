Excersie 6

Delicious Shakshuka

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 4–6 eggs
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish
  • Crusty bread for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onion and bell pepper, cook until soft (about 5 minutes).
  2. Stir in garlic and spices. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in crushed tomatoes, reduce heat, and simmer for 10 minutes until thickened.
  4. Make small wells in the sauce and crack the eggs into them.
  5. Cover the pan and cook for 5–7 minutes until eggs are just set.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and garnish with parsley or cilantro.
  7. Serve hot with bread.
